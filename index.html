Admission Process

Application for Admission at Benguet State University couldn't be simnpler

  • 1

Make sure to create an account HERE first

  • 2

Applicants can now fill up the Applcation Form. Applicants can't proceed until all fields are filled.

  • 3

Submit your duly accomlished Admission form and wait the confirmation sent to your email

  • 4

A Unique Permanent Registration Number will be sent to your email and schedule of your face to face visit at the Benguet State University- La Trinidad Campus. Remember to bring the complete requirements or else you have to resubmit your application form.

  • 5

Keep your lines open while waiting for the announcement of result. You can view the tentative announcement date at the schedule page.

  • 6

You will receive a Notice of Admission (NOA) if you are admitted to the program you choose. If the slots the choosen program reached their required qouta, applicant will recieve Notice of Result (NOR) where you can apply to other programs offered by Benguet State University.
